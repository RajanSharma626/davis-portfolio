About Me

I am first generation from my family that graduated college. My parents moved to the U.S. from Vietnam so that my siblings and I can go to college and and have a good future. My parent's highest education was elementary school. They had to drop out so that they can help their families in the rice fields and farms so that they can provide for their families. I am the second oldest sibling out of four. As for the oldest male besides my father of the household, I had to carry a lot of responsibilities that were given to me. Growing up, I never knew what kind of hobbies I liked because I was too busy helping my parents out with the house, bills, translate from Vietnamese to English, go to doctors appointment, etc. Until my late high school through college years, I started to find certain hobbies that I liked. Growing up, my parents didn't have time to put my siblings and I in extracurricular activities due to of working all the time to provide income. My siblings and I grew up watching ourselves. I am also bilingual so I not only know English, I know Vietnamese. While living under my parents roof, my siblings and I were never allowed to speak English in the house or we would be disciplined. So we only spoke Vietnamese to one another.
